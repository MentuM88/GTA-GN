==========Installation Instructions==========================

**Vehicle Model Install**
Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\x64\dlcpacks\patchday16ng\dlc.rpf\x64\levels\gta5\vehicles.rpf
-Place the yft and ytd files here

**Vehicles Meta Install**
Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\update.rpf\common\data\levels\gta5
-Replace Vehicles Meta here
-Or just replace line in your own meta with the one provided

**Carvariations Meta Install**
Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\x64\dlcpacks\mpimportexport\dlc.rpf\common\data
-Replace Carvariations Meta here
-Or just replace line in your own meta with the one provided

**IF REPLACING OTHER SLOT**

-Rename the model names to name of desired model
--police4.yft
--police4_hi.yft
--police4.ytd

-Rename these POLICE4s to the name of the desired model in the vehicles.meta

     ----->	<modelName>police4</modelName>
     ----->	<txdName>police4</txdName>
      		<handlingId>TEMPESTA</handlingId>
     ----->	<gameName>POLICE4</gameName>

-Rename POLICE4 to the name of the desired model in the carvariations.meta

		<Item>
     ----->	<modelName>POLICE4</modelName>
		<colors>
